Fact of the Day

The best source of fiber for proper toot wear and gut motility is hay. Hay should comprise 75% of a guinea pig's diet.

Leafy greens (lettuces) also provide long stem fiber as well as large volumes of water. Feed 3-5 differnt kinds of leafy greens daily. Leafy green include:

  • Romaine
  • Boston Lettuce
  • Cilantro
  • Dandelion
